# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per year, in addition to UK public holidays.

Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis calculated from the 25-day entitlement.

## Leave Accrual
Leave accrues from your start date. In your first year, you accrue leave monthly at a rate of 2.08 days per month.

## Booking Leave
Leave must be requested via the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for periods under 5 days, and 4 weeks in advance for periods of 5 days or more.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A self-certification form is required for absences of 1-3 days. A doctor's note is required for absences of 4 or more days.

## Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: Up to 52 weeks (26 weeks ordinary, 26 weeks additional)
- Paternity leave: Up to 2 weeks paid
- Shared parental leave: Available after the first 2 weeks of maternity leave

## Carry Over
A maximum of 5 days of unused annual leave may be carried over to the next leave year. Unused leave beyond this is forfeited.

## Compassionate Leave
Up to 5 days of paid compassionate leave is available for the death of an immediate family member.
