# Expenses Policy

## Overview
The company will reimburse reasonable business expenses incurred in the course of your work. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred.

## Meals and Entertainment
- Client meals: Up to £75 per person with prior manager approval
- Team meals: Up to £40 per person, requires manager approval
- Working lunches (internal): Up to £15 per person

## Travel
- Rail travel: Standard class for journeys under 3 hours; first class permitted for journeys over 3 hours with manager approval
- Flights: Economy class for flights under 5 hours; business class requires director approval
- Taxis: Permitted when public transport is impractical. Receipts required for fares over £10.
- Personal vehicle: Reimbursed at HMRC approved rate (currently 45p per mile for first 10,000 miles)

## Accommodation
- London: Up to £200 per night
- Other UK cities: Up to £150 per night
- International: Up to £250 per night, requires manager approval

## Equipment and Software
- Home office equipment: Up to £500 per year with manager approval
- Software subscriptions: Must be pre-approved by your line manager and IT

## Submission
Submit all expenses via the Expenses portal with original receipts. Expenses submitted without receipts will not be reimbursed unless under £25.

## Prohibited Expenses
The following will not be reimbursed: personal entertainment, fines or penalties, alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment event), gym memberships.
