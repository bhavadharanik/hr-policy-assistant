# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a workplace that is inclusive, respectful, and free from harassment and discrimination.

## Expected Behaviour
All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism
- Communicate openly and honestly
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Report concerns or violations promptly

## Prohibited Conduct
The following behaviours are strictly prohibited:
- Harassment or bullying of any kind, including verbal, physical, or online
- Discrimination based on age, gender, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic
- Sharing confidential company or client data without authorisation
- Conflicts of interest that are not disclosed to management
- Use of company resources for personal gain

## Reporting
If you witness or experience a violation of this code, report it to your line manager or HR directly. Anonymous reporting is available via the Ethics Hotline.

## Consequences
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.

## Confidentiality
All reports of misconduct will be treated with confidentiality to the extent possible while conducting a thorough investigation.
